Did you know that wolves have an extraordinary sense of smell?

A wolf's sense of smell is up to 100 times more sensitive than that of humans. They can detect prey from a mile away and can even pick up on scents buried under several feet of snow. This incredible olfactory ability helps them hunt and communicate with other wolves in their pack, playing a crucial role in their survival in the wild.
